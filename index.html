Easy Ride Booking

Book tricycle rides instantly with our user-friendly interface. Select your pickup and destination points with just a few taps.

Fare Estimates

Get accurate fare estimates before booking your ride. Transparent pricing with no hidden charges for peace of mind.

Real-time Trip Tracking

Track your tricycle's location in real-time. Know exactly when your ride will arrive and monitor your journey progress.
